Grading Guidelines

  • Grades are computed and released at the end of each semester
  • Students must maintain a minimum GPA of 2.0 to continue enrollment
  • Incomplete (INC) grades must be removed within one semester
  • Grade appeals must be filed within 5 days of grade posting
  • Academic probation is triggered by GPA below 2.0
